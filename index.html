What is CollectivAI?

CollectivAI is a sovereign, transparent and ethical AI ecosystem designed for the future of humanity. Built on a decentralized network of compute nodes, it empowers citizens, researchers and developers with a secure, democratic and privacy-first AI infrastructure.

Core Principles

  • Ethical & Human-Centered
  • Democratic Governance
  • Privacy by Design
  • Transparent & Auditable
  • Open-Source Infrastructure
  • Secure & Resilient

Architecture Overview

CollectivAI is powered by a distributed global compute network: macOS nodes, Linux nodes, ARM devices, Hackberry CM5, Raspberry Pi, Jetson Orin and cloud-independent infrastructures.

Use Cases

  • Scientific Research & Acceleration
  • Climate Modeling & Environmental Protection
  • Economic Forecasting & Crisis Prevention
  • Healthcare & Medical AI
  • Smart Public Infrastructure
  • Ethics & Governance Systems
