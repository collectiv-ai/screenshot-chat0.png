CollectiVAI Ecosystem

One ecosystem ‚Äì app, router, SDKs & chain

CollectiVAI is more than a single app. It‚Äôs a set of open components that can be combined, extended and audited ‚Äì from prototypes to large-scale civic deployments.

The CollectiVAI App

A democratic AI companion for people, projects and institutions in Europe. One clean, focused interface on top of a multi-provider router ‚Äì no provider dashboard juggling.

  • Chat experience tuned for democracy, policy, research and civic work.
  • Transparent routing view: see which provider answered and why.
  • About view with mission, safeguards and links ‚Äì no black boxes.

CollectiVAI Router

A backend that connects OpenAI, Gemini, Mistral, Meta, DeepSeek and custom models. It decides where to send prompts and wraps every call with ethical rules and logging.

  • No API keys in the app ‚Äì secrets live in the router and infra.
  • Provider selection rules per use case, topic or organisation.
  • Central logging and policy enforcement instead of scattered configs.

CollectiVAI SDKs

Language clients so that existing systems, portals or civic platforms can connect directly to the router and ecosystem.

  • Starting with TypeScript/JavaScript and Python SDKs.
  • Focus on simple, well-documented civic use-case examples.
  • Designed to be integrated into portals, tools and research workflows.

CollectiVAI Chain

A Cosmos-based app-chain concept (pre-alpha, research & design) for transparent governance: proposals, votes, parameters and treasury ‚Äì with a civic, not casino focus.

  • On-chain proposals for rules, parameters and budgets.
  • Transparent decision making and long-term governance.
  • No focus on speculation ‚Äì designed for public-minded infrastructure.

FAQ

Questions people usually ask

A few quick answers. For everything else, just send a mail to info@collectivai.org.

Is there a CollectiVAI token?
No. The focus is on public-minded infrastructure, governance and real-world use cases ‚Äì not on speculative token mechanisms.
Who controls CollectiVAI?
CollectiVAI starts as an independent project and is designed to evolve towards a transparent, democratic governance model, where rules and parameters can be inspected and shaped collectively.
How is privacy handled?
API keys stay in the router and infrastructure, not in the app. The goal is to wrap every provider call with clear ethics, privacy rules and logging ‚Äì so organisations can build on top with confidence.
Can our organisation run its own router or chain node?
Yes, that‚Äôs part of the long-term vision: cities, universities, NGOs and other actors in Europe running their own components ‚Äì interoperable and aligned with shared democratic values.
