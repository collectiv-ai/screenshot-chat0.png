What is CollectivAI?

CollectivAI is a human-centered AI infrastructure that routes your prompts to different AI providers behind a single ethical and transparent control layer. The goal: AI that helps people – in science, economy, climate and society – instead of creating more chaos.

CollectivAI App · Key Features

One interface – many AI backends. Focus on decisions and research, not on switching tools.

Multi-Backend Routing

Plug in different AI providers (e.g. Gemini, GPT, custom APIs) and route each prompt to the best backend based on task, cost and quality.

Ethical & Privacy Layer

Define rules for what data is allowed to leave your ecosystem. Protect sensitive content while still using powerful models.

Unified Interface

One clean UI for research, coding, economics, climate models, security analysis and more – on MacBook, desktop and mobile.

Architecture Overview

Behind the app, CollectivAI uses a routing layer that speaks to multiple AI APIs. Your MacBook or iPhone acts as the control panel; the heavy lifting happens on external AI backends, always through your ethical and privacy rules.
