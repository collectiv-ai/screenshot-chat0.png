What is CollectivAI?

CollectivAI is a human-centered AI ecosystem built on a distributed network of compute nodes. It empowers citizens, researchers and developers with an ethical, transparent and privacy-first AI infrastructure.

Instead of concentrating power in a few companies, CollectivAI aims to create an open, verifiable and democratic foundation for AI – designed to support society, not control it.

Core Principles

Ethical & Human-Centered

AI must protect people, improve life and respect human dignity. CollectivAI is built to help, not harm.

Democratic Governance

Citizens, experts and developers share responsibility for how AI is built, deployed and supervised.

Privacy by Design

Data belongs to the individual. CollectivAI prioritizes encryption, consent and minimal data usage.

Transparent & Auditable

Models, decisions and updates should be explainable, reviewable and open for public scrutiny.

Open-Source Infrastructure

The core of CollectivAI is open, allowing researchers and developers to verify, extend and improve it.

Secure & Resilient

Designed to withstand failures, attacks and misuse through layered security and robust architecture.

Architecture Overview

CollectivAI is powered by a distributed compute network: macOS nodes, Linux servers, ARM systems, Hackberry CM5, Raspberry Pi, Jetson Orin and cloud-independent infrastructures.

Layered Design

  • Compute Layer: Diverse hardware nodes running AI workloads.
  • Model Layer: Transparent, explainable and auditable AI models.
  • Governance Layer: Transparent voting and decision-making.
  • Data Layer: Federated, encrypted and consent-based data usage.
  • Interface Layer: APIs, dashboards and tools for humans and systems.
